While embarking on this walk, it started getting reallllllllly weird. I frequently had hallucinations of friends (who were in totally different cities than me) conversing and making plans, and when I would look back to talk to them, they were gone. I also had an extreme paranoid feeling.

Some of the side effects included extreme dry mouth, irritated eyes, a verrrry bad taste, and my vision was so horrible I couldn't read a phone number I had written in my wallet. So again, if you play on taking brugmansia, DO NOT take too much. Thats pretty much all to my story, so have fun if you decide to experiment with it.
